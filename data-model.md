# LUMNAI Research Data Model

## Purpose

This model defines abstract variables used to analyze system-level interactions in complex environments without referencing real-world clinical or operational implementations.

---

## Input Variables

- System parameters (generalized)
- Procedural factors (abstract representation)
- Contextual conditions

---

## Response Variables

- System behavior under change
- Variability patterns
- Stability indicators

---

## External Variables

- Environmental influences
- Context shifts
- External constraints

---

## Output Variables

- Final system state
- Variability index
- Pattern consistency

---

## Core Relationship

Outcome is determined by the interaction of:

Input × Response × External conditions

---

## Scope

This model is strictly conceptual and does not describe or replicate any real-world clinical, medical, or operational system.

---

## Purpose

To support abstract system modeling and research into complexity-driven outcome variability.
